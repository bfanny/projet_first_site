Frogs and their myths

The Frog who could read in the sky

It is said that frogs have an ability, a power hidden for centuries. In effect frogs locked in jars would be able to predict the weather!

  • If the frog stays in the bottom and kicks, a nice rain is given..
  • If the frog goes up the sun is announced!

This is obviously only a nice account, but who knows ...

The Frogs in History

In the Middle Ages, toads were described as containing a crapaudine. The crapaudine is a mystic stone that allowed the healing of all types of poisons. If in the Middle East the frog predicted time, in Europe it was associated with healing.

The frog is a symbolizing animal:

  • Purification
  • Renewal, rebirth
  • Fertility and abundance
  • Transformation, methamorphosis
  • The connection with the mysteries of life and ancient wisdom
